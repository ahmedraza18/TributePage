Mahendra Singh Dhoni

The man who made India Proud

Only captain to win 3 big trophies for India, 2007 ICC t20 world cup, 2011 ICC ODI world cup, 2013 Champion trophy.

Here's a timeline of Mahi's Life:

Domestic Career

  • 1981 - Born in Ranchi, India
  • 1999-2004 - Played for Bihar cricket Team
  • 2004-present - Played for Jharkhand
  • 2008-2015 - Captain of Chennai Super Kings in IPL, and won the title for two consecutive years 2010 and 2011 and also won Champions League Twenty20 in 2010 and 2014.
  • 2016-2017 - Come up with a new team Rising Pune Supergiant since CSK was banned from IPL for two years, that was not so good for Mahi, they were unable to make in to playoffs with that team.
  • 2018-present - CSK were back in the game after two years with the same look and feel in the team Mahi was back in to the squad. They were able to lift the cup once again in 2018 by beating SRH by 8 wickets.

International Career

  • 2004- Debut in India Squad against Bangladesh (cap 158)
  • 2005 - Test Cricket Debut against Sri Lanka (cap 251)
  • 2007 - Won the ICC t20I World Cup with the young squad beating pakistan in the final in South Africa.
  • 2011 - Won the ICC International world cup in against Sri Lanka in Mumbai. Scored 91 runs and won the match by hitting a six finishing off in Mahi's style.
  • 2013 - Won the ICC Champions trophy beating england in their Hometown.
  • 2014 - Played his Last Test against Australia
  • 2019- Last T20I against Australia.
  • 2019 - Last world Cup and last ODI match against New Zeland. MS has given his best but than also India Lost.
  • 15 Aug 2020 - MS has announced his retirement from International Cricket.
